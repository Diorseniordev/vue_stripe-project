Tokens

What are tokens?

Book your flights using tokens. One token equals a one-way flight.

What happens to my unused tokens?

Depending on your subscription plan, you will get up to one or two tokens that will rollover onto the next month.

Can I share or send my tokens

Yes, tokens can be shared or sent. All that has to happen is the traveler you want to send or share tokens with has to have a free Wanderift account!
