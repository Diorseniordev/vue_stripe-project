Frequently asked questions

When Will I Recieve My Flight Confirmation

Shortly after confirming your booking, you will receive an email from Wanderift with your confirmation number and flight details.

What happens to my unused tokens?

Depending on your subscription plan, you will be able to rollover a certain number of tokens. With Lite, you can rollover up to ONE unused token. With Pro, you can roll over up toTWO unused tokens.

Can I share or send my tokens

Yes, your tokens can be shared or sent. All that has to happen is the traveler who you want to share or send your tokens has to create a free Wanderift account.
