About Us

Our Story

Wanderift is airline travel as a subscription. At Wanderift, we want to accomplish three simple goals; make frequent travel simple, inexpensive, & fun. We achieve these goals through our subscription model. Our model allows travelers to fly frequently at an affordable rate. Add in our simple booking process, and a new and exciting model is brought to the travel industry!

The origin of Wanderift has humble beginnings. While attending Mount St. Mary’s University, our founder, Zach, noticed the value subscription models bring to the consumer, and realized one of the few industries lacking a subscription model was the airline industry. After much research, Wanderift was formed. The name Wanderift comes from two words, ‘Wander’ our subscribers ‘wander’ the country for the same price and ‘rift’ because of the ‘rift’ we are causing in the industry. Zach left school and moved to Dallas to build Wanderift. He has always had a strong passion for the travel industry, specifically the airline industry and our team shares that passion.
